PING-PONG

Rules

Ping-Pong counts up to a number with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by three become 'ping'
  2. Numbers divisible by five become 'pong'
  3. Numbers divisible by fifteen become 'ping-pong'
  4. Numbers divisible by eighteen return a surprise
